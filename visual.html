Total reflection and Brewster's angle

Yirui Gao, School of Information
This is the work for individual project in SI649, Information Visualization
For this visualization demo, the upper part I show is a simulation of wave in both polarizations. You will be able to use the control panel to drag or input the values for each variable. The lower three charts are plotting relationships between incidence angle and refracted angle, power reflection coefficient, power transmission coefficient. The curves are also based on what you input.


Hope you have fun and learn something here!
